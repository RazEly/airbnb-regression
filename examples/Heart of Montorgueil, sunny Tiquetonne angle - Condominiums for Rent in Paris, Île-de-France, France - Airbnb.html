Neighborhood highlights

The Montorgueil district is located in the center of Paris, the shopping street that bears its name connects Parc des Halles and the Sentier district.
It is a very popular area, as it has many cafes, bars and restaurants.
A multitude of shops and businesses specializing in gourmet products complete this particularly lively area.
On the other side of Parc des Halles, La Samaritaine and Pont-Neuf are in line with Rue Montorgueil.
Rue Étienne Marcel is perpendicular and joins on one side rue du Louvre, which leads to the museum of the same name as well as to the Seine, or even by continuing along the street, Place des Victoires, along the garden of the Palais-Royal, passes through the Vivienne district, to Boulevard de l'Opéra.
In the other direction, Rue Étienne Marcel leads to the Pompidou Center and the Marais district.
